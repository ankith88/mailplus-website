Express delivery

Our express service gets your parcels anywhere in Australia in 1–2 business days, with 95% of shipments arriving overnight on business days and flat-rate pricing for items up to 5kg. Your local owner-operator collects the same day when you book before the daily cut-off.

Pickup is handled by a local owner-operator in your area — a network of approximately 300 drivers covering all major cities, including Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth and Adelaide, plus selected regional areas. From there, our partnerships with leading carriers carry your parcel the rest of the way, so you get reliable 1–2 day delivery anywhere in the country.

Even though carriers handle the line-haul, MailPlus stays your single point of contact — we look after the tracking and customer service for every shipment, so you're never left chasing a third party. There's no lock-in contract and no minimum volume, so the service suits businesses shipping a few parcels a week or several hundred.

It all works hand in hand with ShipMate, our free shipping platform — book pickups, print labels in a few clicks, and manage every order in one dashboard. Plug it straight into Shopify or WooCommerce, or just use it on its own. MailPlus is a reliable, independent alternative to Australia Post and traditional couriers.
