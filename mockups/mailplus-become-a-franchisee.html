The opportunity

A MailPlus franchise is a local, owner-operated courier business within MailPlus's Australian express parcel delivery network — founded in 1997, with 120+ franchises and 300+ owner-operators across Australia.

As a MailPlus franchisee you run an exclusive territory: collecting and lodging parcels and mail for local businesses, clearing PO Boxes, handling Australia Post items, and delivering express parcels — the same connected services MailPlus is known for. There's no shopfront and no retail trade; it's a mobile, B2B business built on recurring customers.

It's a proven, resilient model in the essential and growing e-commerce logistics sector, backed daily by head-office sales, marketing, operational and customer-service teams — plus direct access to founder and Managing Director Chris Burgess. Territories are currently available across NSW, the ACT, QLD, VIC and TAS.

Because each territory is exclusive, you're not competing with another MailPlus operator for the same customers — you build and keep your own base. Day to day, that means same-day collections, Post Office collect & lodge, and express deliveries for the businesses in your area, with the free ShipMate platform handling labels, bookings and tracking so the admin stays light.

It suits owner-operators who want to be their own boss without starting from scratch, and investors looking for a stable business with recurring B2B income. Whether you already have logistics experience or are changing careers entirely, the head-office team helps you profile your territory and grow your customer base from day one.
